Hello! To log on to Ohio University's Wi-Fi:

1.  Select the **OHIO-WIFI** network from your device's Wi-Fi settings.
2.  Enter your **Ohio ID** (e.g., ab123456) and **password** when prompted.
3.  If asked, accept the security certificate.

You should then be connected!

 ---

Hello there! If you need to reset your Ohio University password, you can easily do so through the account management portal.

1.  Go to [**account.ohio.edu**](https://account.ohio.edu).
2.  Click on the "**Forgot Password**" link.
3.  Follow the prompts to verify your identity and set a new password.

If you have any trouble, feel free to reach out to the OIT Service Desk!
