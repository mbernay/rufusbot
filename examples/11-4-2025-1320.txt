Connecting to the Ohio University Wi-Fi is easy!

1.  Select the **`eduroam`** network from your device's Wi-Fi settings.
2.  When prompted, enter your **full OHIO ID** (e.g., `js123417@ohio.edu`) and your **OHIO password**.
3.  You may need to accept a security certificate.

For detailed guides, visit help.ohio.edu and search for "eduroam."
